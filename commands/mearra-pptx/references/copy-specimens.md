# Mearra Copy Specimens — Approved Lines You Can Lift

These lines come straight from the Mearra Brand Identity 2026 PDF or have been written in that voice. Use them verbatim on cover slides, section dividers, and closings. For new copy, use them as models — not filler.

See `tone-of-voice.md` for rules on writing new copy.

## Phrases and words Mearra uses

Lean into: *hard stuff, tackle, solve, dismantle, unpack, outcome, impact, layer by layer, evolve, adapt, flow, in motion, human, hands-on, partner, together, grit, depth, ambition, bar-raising.*

---

## Phrases to avoid (anti-patterns)

Avoid: *solutions, synergies, leverage, paradigm, seamless, holistic, end-to-end, best-in-class, revolutionary, game-changing, world-class, industry-leading, cutting-edge.*

These are generic consultancy-speak. Mearra is specific, active, and confident — it doesn't need superlatives.

## Four go-to deck openings

**1. The confident pitch opening**
> "Complexity gets us going."
> Subtitle: A Mearra concept for [Client] — [Presenters] — [Date]

**2. The collaborative / partnership opening**
> "Between the layers of data, there's always a human."
> Subtitle: [Client] × Mearra — [Quarter] update

**3. The capability opening (all-hands, internal)**
> "Playing tech as our instrument."
> Subtitle: [Team / initiative] — Q[X] review

**4. The steering-group opening**
> "[Project name]. Where we are. What's next."
> Subtitle: Steering group — [Date]
